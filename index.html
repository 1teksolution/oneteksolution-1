About One Tek Solution

One Tek Solution is a premier B2B global sourcing partner and strategic supplier specializing in high-grade semiconductor components, microcontrollers, embedded systems, RF devices, and industrial electronics. Established to bridge the gap in the fast-paced electronics manufacturing industry, we cater extensively to Original Equipment Manufacturers (OEMs), Electronic Manufacturing Services (EMS), system integrators, and independent engineering teams across the country.

In an era where component shortages and supply chain disruptions can halt production, One Tek Solution stands as a reliable pillar of procurement support. We leverage an extensive, deeply vetted international network of trusted manufacturers and authorized distributors to source not only high-volume standard components but also highly specialized, obsolete, allocation-phase, and hard-to-find active or passive semiconductor parts.

Our core product portfolio spans across automotive-grade MCUs, advanced sensors, wireless communication modules, power management ICs, and custom electronic flashers. Driven by a commitment to uncompromising quality, every component supplied by us undergoes rigorous source-verification to ensure 100% authenticity and compliance with global industrial standards, effectively eliminating the risk of counterfeit parts.

Beyond hardware distribution, we take pride in our agility and customer-centric technical assistance. Whether our clients require flexible Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ) support for initial prototyping, complete Bill of Materials (BOM) kitting assistance for large-scale production, or scheduled buffer-stock management for just-in-time delivery, our dedicated team ensures a seamless, transparent, and cost-effective procurement experience from inquiry to final delivery.
