About One Tek Solution

A premier B2B global sourcing partner for the electronics industry.

One Tek Solution is a premier B2B global sourcing partner specializing in high-grade semiconductor components, microcontrollers, embedded systems, RF devices, and industrial electronics. We cater to OEMs, EMS providers, system integrators, and engineering teams across India.

We leverage an extensive, vetted international network to source standard, obsolete, and hard-to-find semiconductor parts. Every component undergoes rigorous source-verification to ensure 100% authenticity and compliance, eliminating the risk of counterfeit parts.

Whether you need flexible MOQ support for prototyping, complete BOM kitting for large-scale production, or scheduled buffer-stock management — our dedicated team ensures a seamless, transparent, and cost-effective procurement experience.
