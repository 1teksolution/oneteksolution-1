Who We Are

About One Tek Solution

A premier B2B sourcing, import, and electronics manufacturing company headquartered in New Delhi.

One Tek Solution is a premier B2B company specializing in global semiconductor sourcing, component import, and in-house electronics manufacturing. We serve OEMs, EMS providers, system integrators, Indian Railways, automobile companies, and engineering teams across India.

Led by MD K. Chaudhary and Technical Director A. Kumar, our team brings deep expertise across R&D, Sales, Import, and Manufacturing — delivering end-to-end solutions under one roof.

We leverage an extensive, vetted international network to source standard, obsolete, and hard-to-find semiconductor parts. Every component is source-verified for 100% authenticity, eliminating counterfeit risk. Our manufacturing division produces certified electronic products for automobile, fire safety, power, and railway sectors.
